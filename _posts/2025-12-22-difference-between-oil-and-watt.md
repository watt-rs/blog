Initially, the project started as a simple interpreted, lightweight, dynamically typed language on Rust. 
Under this concept, the project developed for about 9 months without any major conceptual or philosophical changes, 
quite successfully. But development does not stand still. Evolution is inevitable.

*Some time ago (about 4 months)*, the concept and philosophy of the *Watt language* was radically revised, 
and the launch of the statically typed programming language *Oil* for the Javascript platform was announced.
